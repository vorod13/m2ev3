About Mindset2Excel

Real-world experience. Structured thinking. No generic advice.

Anand Nagarajan

Founder & Lead Coach | ICF Certified Coach | Leadership & Transition Specialist | 2x Founder | Motivational Speaker

I've lived through every stage I now coach — navigating insecurity, building confidence as a leader, and making the leap from a safe corporate path to entrepreneurship. Each step, with its failures and breakthroughs, taught me that real growth comes from honest reflection and tested mindset shifts.

All of this happened while building a family, raising kids, and making sure I still did the things that brought me joy and purpose along the way. Mindset2Excel was created from these lived experiences. If I can make these changes, I truly believe anyone can.

The programs are built around the same reflection tools and questions that helped me reinvent my own life and career — while balancing the demands of work, family, and personal growth. This isn't theory. It's proven practice, grounded in real experience.

Our Approach
